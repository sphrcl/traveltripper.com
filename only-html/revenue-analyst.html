Revenue Analyst

Travel Tripper About Us Careers Revenue Analyst

Job Opening: Revenue Analyst

The Revenue Analyst position is primarily responsible for generating online reservations for Travel Tripper’s customers in order to meet and exceed revenue targets and establish a market presence. The job requires a combination of revenue management and tactical online marketing. He/She must also assist Client services team to serve as a point of contact for all the client’s needs from relationship building, customer-service and championing the customer’s product enhancement requirements.

About Travel Tripper

Travel Tripper is fast growing and dynamic software company based in Manhattan and serving the hotel industry. Our team brings together expertise in the marketing, technology, and hospitality sectors. We work together collaboratively to create, refine, and then shape new ideas into great products for our customers.

Working at Travel Tripper is an opportunity to join an exciting company with a great team culture and a successful track record. We are highly selective in our hiring process seeking individuals with demonstrated excellence in their backgrounds. We look for smart people who are passionate about technology and the web, who care deeply about their careers, and who want to work hard and have fun.

We offer a collaborative and interdependent team environment where you can apply your experience in building world class software across all tiers of our system. We offer full benefits including a Options Plan, Medical, Dental, Vision, 401(k), three weeks vacation, and more.

Responsibilities

  • Understand and internalize Travel Tripper’s products and services
  • Support client services managers with account management of clients
  • Ensure proper implementation of products for new customer hotels
  • Provide support for client support requests across all products
  • Diagnose and duplicate customer reported issuesand manage the resolution of the problem – including ability to communicate with technical team to troubleshoot
  • Develop and maintain relationships with customers
  • Educate customers on Travel Tripper products and new feature releases
  • Assist in generation and distribution of weekly and monthly reports to clients
  • Analyze performance metrics in reports distributed to clients
  • Communicate and follow up with internal teams for enhancement requests, escalated support requests, and marketing inquiries
  • Maintain high level of professionalism when interacting with clients
  • Assist with ad hoc projects as needed to provide client satisfaction and company growth

Requirements

  • A Bachelor’s Degree or managerial/supervisory experience in the Hospitality or Travel industry
  • Strong and proven track record in hospitality revenue management or reservations, or other industry with fixed, perishable inventory
  • Demonstrate ability to work based on targets/goals
  • Excellent verbal and written communications skills
  • High interest in working for a fast paced technology firm
  • Very comfortable with large volumes of data and spreadsheets and an ability to perform “back of the envelope” style calculations on the fly
  • 2 years of travel industry experience (hospitality experience preferred)
  • Proficiency in Microsoft Office with particularly VERY strong knowledge of Excel required
  • Ability to multi task and prioritize effectively while possessing excellent time management and organizational skills
  • Detail oriented and highly organized
